# Digital Marketing Campaign Analysis

## Project Overview

This project performs Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and visualization on a digital marketing campaign dataset to understand customer behavior, campaign effectiveness, and conversion performance. The analysis aims to identify key drivers of customer conversions and provide actionable business recommendations.

## Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics and engagement behavior.
* Evaluate the effectiveness of different marketing channels and campaign types.
* Identify factors influencing customer conversion.
* Visualize key performance metrics using Power BI.
* Generate business insights and recommendations for marketing optimization.

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains 8,000 customer records with information related to:

* Customer demographics (Age, Gender)
* Campaign channels and campaign types
* Advertising spend
* Website engagement metrics
* Email marketing metrics
* Loyalty and purchase history
* Conversion status

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

### Data Preprocessing

* Imported and explored the dataset.
* Checked for missing values and duplicates.
* Performed descriptive statistical analysis.

### Analysis Performed

1. Conversion Distribution Analysis
2. Marketing Channel Performance Analysis
3. Campaign Type Performance Analysis
4. Ad Spend vs Conversion Analysis
5. Website Engagement Analysis

   * Website Visits
   * Pages Per Visit
   * Time on Site
6. Email Marketing Performance Analysis

   * Email Opens
   * Email Clicks
7. Customer Demographic Analysis

   * Age Distribution
   * Gender Analysis
8. Loyalty and Previous Purchase Analysis
9. Correlation Analysis
10. Marketing Funnel Analysis

## Tools and Technologies

* Python

## Key Insights

* Customer engagement metrics showed a strong relationship with conversion outcomes.
* Email interaction significantly influenced conversion rates.
* Marketing channels varied in effectiveness, highlighting opportunities for budget optimization.
* Customers with previous purchases and higher loyalty scores were more likely to convert.
* Funnel analysis revealed customer drop-off points that can be targeted for improvement.

## Power BI Dashboard

The dashboard was developed to provide an interactive view of:

* Total Customers
* Total Conversions
* Conversion Rate
* Total Ad Spend
* Conversion by Marketing Channel
* Conversion by Campaign Type
* Customer Demographics
* Engagement Metrics
* Marketing Funnel Analysis

## Recommendations

* Allocate more resources to high-performing marketing channels.
* Improve email marketing personalization and engagement.
* Optimize website experience to increase customer interaction.
* Strengthen customer retention and loyalty programs.
* Address funnel drop-off stages through targeted marketing strategies.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be used to evaluate marketing campaign performance and identify opportunities to improve customer engagement and conversion rates. The insights generated can support data-driven decision-making and marketing strategy optimization.
